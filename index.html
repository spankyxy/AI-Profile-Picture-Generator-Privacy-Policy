Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AI Headshot Generator ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner.

Important Notice About Face Data: Our app collects and processes photos containing facial data to generate AI headshots. Please read the "Face Data Collection and Use" section carefully to understand how we handle this sensitive information.

1. Face Data Collection and Use

1.1 What Face Data We Collect

When you use our App, we collect the following face-related data:

  • Photographs containing faces: Users voluntarily upload photos that contain their faces or the faces of others (with appropriate consent) to generate AI-powered professional headshots.
  • No biometric extraction: We do NOT extract, store, or process any specific facial features, facial recognition data, biometric identifiers, or facial measurements. The images are processed as complete photographs only.

1.2 How We Use Face Data

The face data collected is used exclusively for the following purpose:

  • AI Headshot Generation: Your uploaded photos are sent to our third-party AI processing provider (Fal AI using the Nano Banana model) to generate professional AI headshots based on your input image. The AI analyzes the photo to create new, styled headshot images.
  • No other uses: We do NOT use your face data for:
    • Training or improving AI models
    • Marketing or advertising purposes
    • Facial recognition or identification
    • Analytics or tracking
    • Sharing with third parties (except as described in section 1.3)
    • Any purpose other than generating your requested headshots

1.3 Face Data Sharing with Third Parties

Third-Party AI Processor: Your uploaded photos are shared with our AI processing provider:

  • Provider: Fal AI (https://fal.ai/)
  • AI Model: Nano Banana
  • Purpose: To process your images and generate AI headshots
  • Data Handling: Fal AI processes your images in accordance with their privacy policy and security practices

No Other Third-Party Sharing: Your face data is NOT shared with any other third parties, advertisers, data brokers, or external services. We do not sell, rent, or trade your face data.

1.4 Face Data Storage and Retention

Uploaded Photos (Original Images):

  • App Storage: We do NOT store your uploaded photos on our servers or in any cloud storage (including Firebase Storage).
  • Temporary Processing: Your uploaded photos are transmitted directly to Fal AI for processing and are not retained by our App.
  • AI Provider Retention: Fal AI automatically deletes your uploaded photos from their servers after following processing.

Generated Headshots (AI Output Images):

  • Storage Duration: The AI-generated headshot images are stored on our servers for 1 year from the date of generation.
  • Automatic Deletion: After 30 days, all generated headshots are automatically and permanently deleted from our systems.
  • User-Initiated Deletion: You can request earlier deletion of your generated headshots at any time (see section 1.5).

1.5 Your Rights Regarding Face Data

You have the following rights concerning your face data:

  • Access: You can request information about what face data we have processed.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your uploaded photos and generated headshots at any time by contacting us via email.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You can stop using the App at any time, and no further face data will be collected.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided in the "Contact Us" section below.
